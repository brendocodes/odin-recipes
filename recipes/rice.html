Egg Fried Rice

Recipe Overview:

This is my go to recipe for a quick bowl of egg fried rice at home.

  • Prep: 15 Minutes
  • Cook: 15 Minutes
  • Serves: 1

Ingredients

  • 1/4 cup dry white jasmine rice, cooked the night before and refrigerated overnight
  • 1 tsp extra virgin olive oil, or any neutral tasting oil
  • 1/2 tsp soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp toasted sesame oil
  • 1 large egg, whisked in a bowl
  • Salt and pepper, to taste

Optional Ingredients

  • 1 clove of garlic, minced
  • 1 small bunch of spring onion, sliced for garnish
  • 1 medium shallot or a quarter of a medium white onion, diced
  • 1/4 cup vegetable of your choosing, I like to add fresh carrot, diced
  • 1/2 inch knob of ginger, peeled and grated
  • 1/2 tbsp unsalted butter
  • 1/4 cup rinsed bean sprouts
  • 1 pinch of red pickled ginger, to garnish
  • 1/2 tsp toasted sesame seeds, to garnish
  • 4 pieces of organic tofu, cut bite sized and deep fried
  • Sriracha and/or kewpie mayonnaise as garnish to taste

Steps:

  1. Heat a medium wok or frying pan over medium-low heat
  2. Add oil and butter to season the pan
  3. While the pan gets hot prep your vegetables
  4. With your veggies prepped and your pan hot, add the garlic and ginger and let the spices brown until fragrant (2 minutes)
  5. Add veggies and saute until softened for 2 minutes
  6. Add your cold rice to the pan and break apart with a spatula until everything is mixed evenly
  7. Now add the whisked egg and season the rice with soy, sesame oil, salt and pepper. Mix evenly
  8. Add bean sprouts and mix into the rice
  9. When rice is evenly mixed and appearing golden brown in color, transfer to a bowl
  10. Top with fried tofu, scallions, toasted sesame seeds and other garnish
  11. Enjoy a nice, warm bowl of hearty egg fried rice!
