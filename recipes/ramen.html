Spicy Tonkotsu Ramen

Recipe Overview:

This is my go to recipe for a quick bowl of ramen at home.

  • Prep: 15 Minutes
  • Cook: 15 Minutes
  • Serves: 1

Ingredients:

  • 1 bowl or packet of instant ramen, I use Nongshim Spicy Tonkotsu Ramen

Optional Ingredients:

  • 1 tsp extra virgin olive oil, or any neutral tasting oil
  • 1/2 tsp soy sauce
  • 1/2 tsp toasted sesame oil
  • 1/2 tbsp unsalted butter, for added richness
  • 1 clove of garlic, minced
  • 1 medium shallot or a quarter of any medium onion, diced
  • 1/4 cup diced vegetable of your choosing, I like to add fresh carrot
  • 1 egg, softboiled if preferred
  • 4 pieces of organic tofu, cut bite sized
  • 2 strips of bacon, diced
  • 4 pieces of frozen, shaved beef chuck
  • 1 stalk of spring onion, thinly sliced for garnish
  • 1/2 tsp toasted sesame seeds, sprinkled on top for garnish

Steps:

  1. Heat a medium saucepan over medium-low heat
  2. While the pan gets hot prep your vegetables
  3. With your veggies prepped and your pan hot, add the oil and butter
  4. Add the onion and any other veggies to the pot and saute the veggies for 2 minutes
  5. While the veggies soften, dice the bacon and throw it in along with the garlic
  6. At this point we are going to turn the heat up to high
  7. Now you can add any seasoning packets that come with the instant ramen, as well as the soy and sesame oil
  8. Stir constantly for two minutes on high heat, then add the recommended amount of water for your instant ramen.
  9. Cover the saucepan and let come to a rolling boil
  10. Once boiling add any other protein to the pot, including tofu, beef, and egg (I like to carefully crack and drop a raw egg into the boiling broth for a runny yolk)
  11. After letting your proteins cook for a minute, add your noodles and cover again
  12. Noodle cook time will vary depending on packaging, but at a rolling boil most should be done in under 2 minutes for al dente
  13. Transfer noodles and broth into a bowl and top with any garnish
  14. Enjoy a nice, filling bowl of ramen noodle soup!

Ramen Egg Recipe:

If you wish to go a step further, here is a recipe for perfect soft boiled ramen eggs.

Ingredients:

  • 6 large eggs
  • 1 tbsp vinegar
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup mirin
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/4 cup water

Steps:

  1. Bring a large saucepan filled with water to a rolling boil over high heat
  2. Add the vinegar and carefully lower the eggs into the water
  3. Set a timer for 7 minutes 30 seconds
  4. While the eggs boil prepare a bowl of ice water
  5. Drain saucepan and immediately submerge eggs in ice water bath to stop cooking once timer finishes
  6. While eggs cool, prepare a tupperware container or deep mixing bowl with the egg marinade
  7. Add soy, mirin, sugar, and water to container and whisk until combined
  8. Carefully crack the cooled eggs, peeling the shells off
  9. Submerge peeled eggs in marinade overnight and enjoy in your next bowl of ramen!
