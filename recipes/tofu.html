Deep Fried Age Tofu

Recipe Overview:

This is my go to recipe for a quick snack of age tofu at home.

  • Prep: 5 Minutes
  • Cook: 10 Minutes
  • Serves: 1

Ingredients

  • 1 14oz package of organic tofu, cut into bite sized blocks
  • 3 cups canola or vegetable oil, for frying
  • 1 cup corn starch
  • 1/4 cup tempura sauce
  • 1/2 inch knob of fresh ginger, peeled and grated
  • 1 small bunch of spring onion, sliced for garnish

Steps:

  1. Heat a medium saucepan over medium heat
  2. Add oil and bring to frying temperature, 300°-350°F
  3. While the oil gets hot cut your tofu, pat dry with a paper towel and add to a large mixing bowl with cornstarch
  4. Carefully lower battered tofu into hot oil using a slotted spoon, in batches of 6-8 so that tofu is completely submerged
  5. Gently fry for 6-7 minutes, stirring occasionally to prevent tofu sticking together
  6. Remove fried tofu and set aside on a plate lined with paper towels to dry and drain excess oil
  7. Repeat with remaining tofu until tofu is fried and set out to dry
  8. While tofu dries, heat a small skillet over low heat
  9. Add grated ginger and tempura sauce until warm and steamy
  10. Plate fried age tofu in a bowl and drizzle warm tempura sauce over top, coating each piece completely
  11. Top with sliced spring onion and enjoy a savory bowl of crunchy age tofu!

Tempura Sauce:

If you wish to go a step further, here is a recipe for savory home-made tempura sauce.

Ingredients:

  • 1 tbsp hondashi powder or equivalent bonito soup stock flavoring
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 1/4 cup soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup mirin

Steps:

  1. Bring a small saucepan to temperature over medium-low heat
  2. Add the soy and mirin
  3. Simmer for 2-3 minutes to cook off the alcohol from the mirin
  4. Whisk in the sugar and hondashi powder
  5. Serve warm or store in an airtight container for later
